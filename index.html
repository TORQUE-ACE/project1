What is FiveM?

FiveM is a modification for Grand Theft Auto V, enabling you to play on customized multiplayer servers. It allows you to create and join dedicated servers with custom scripts, vehicles, and game modes. With FiveM, you can enjoy a personalized multiplayer experience outside of the standard GTA Online environment.

Features of FiveM

  • Custom multiplayer servers with unique game modes.
  • Advanced modding support for custom vehicles, maps, and scripts.
  • High server performance with support for large player counts.
  • Active community and regular updates.
  • Ability to play with friends on private servers or public ones.

How to Install FiveM

Follow these steps to install FiveM on your computer:

  1. Download the FiveM installer from the official website: FiveM.net
  2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. Launch FiveM and sign in with your Rockstar Social Club account.
  4. Browse and join any server that interests you or start your own.
